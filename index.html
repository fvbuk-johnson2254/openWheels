About openWheels


openWheels is a fan project aimed at deobfuscating the JavaScript port of the beloved physics game Happy Wheels. This effort is purely for educational and archival purposes and is not affiliated with Fancy Force or Total Jerkface.

This website is styled as a homage to the original Total Jerkface experience. We love Happy Wheels and hope to help keep it alive through community efforts.
